Andrew DeMaria

Andrew DeMaria is a senior at Colorado School of Mines in Golden, Colorado studying Computer Science. He spent 3 years teaching Cub Scouts at Peaceful Valley Ranch as a lifeguard. Afterwards, he was an intern for Lockheed Martin under their classified Military Support Program (MSP) where he worked on implementing a full Kerberos and LDAP based Authentication and Authorization system. There began his interest in information security and he has continued diving into the topic since.

In his spare time, Andrew enjoys the Colorado Wilderness with backpacking, climbing, mountaineering, skiing and photography. When the weather does not cooperate he likes to hack on a Raspberry Pi.
